### 1. Customize — `An app that becomes you.` (eyebrow: Yours)

Five-step scroll story. Shared section title; each step has its own copy and a user-prompt `mockTitle` shown above the mock.

| Step | Eyebrow | Title | Body | Mock title (user prompt) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| intro | Yours | An app that becomes you. | Make Stella look how you want. Tabs, a dashboard, a whole new app — just ask, and she becomes it. | Show me what you can do |
| header | Layout | Reshape the chrome. | Ask for tabs and they show up. Stella rearranges the window to match the way you work. | Give me tabs at the top |
| messages | Surfaces | Turn chat into a dashboard. | When a conversation outgrows the chat, Stella turns it into a workspace — your day, laid out at a glance. | Make me a dashboard |

| cozy | Mood | Or a whole new mood. | Stella isn't just an interface — she's an atmosphere. Ask for cozy, calm, or playful, and she becomes it from background to font. | Make a cozy home for my cat |

### 2. Chat — `One chat. From anywhere.` (eyebrow: Always there)

No tabs, no new windows for every idea. Stella keeps one running conversation — pop her open in a small floating window on top of whatever you're doing, ask for two things at once, and she runs them in parallel right inside the same chat.

### 3. Memory — `Stella, who remembers.` (eyebrow: Memory)

Most assistants meet you for the first time, every time. Stella carries what matters — the people, the places, the way you like things — so the next conversation starts where the last one ended.

### 4. Actions — `Stella does the clicking.` (eyebrow: Actions)

Reserve the table, fill the form, ship the email. She drives your computer the way you would — you just say what you want, then watch her do it.

### 5. Voice — `Talk. Or dictate anywhere.` (eyebrow: Voice)

Have a real conversation, hands-free. Or pull up a dictation bar inside any app — mail, messages, the doc you're writing — and just say the next sentence.

### 6. Anywhere — `Text Stella from anywhere.` (eyebrow: Anywhere)

Message her from iMessage, Slack, Discord, Telegram, Teams, or her own app on your phone. She replies from your computer, with all your stuff.

### 7. Models — `Bring any model.` (eyebrow: Models)

Use GPT, Claude, Gemini, or a model running on your own machine. Switch whenever you want — Stella works with all of them.

### 8. Private — `Your stuff stays yours.` (eyebrow: Private)

Chats and files live on your computer. We don't keep them, and we can't see them. Every line of Stella's code is on GitHub — read it, fork it, change it.

## Additional features section (removed)

Eyebrow: **More**
Heading: **Additional features**

- **Browser extension** — Stella rides along in Chrome, ready to read or act on the page you're on.
- **Use your computer** — Open apps, click, type, and get things done on your computer.
- **Collaborate with friends** — Invite people in, create together, and share what you make.
- **Connect your life** — Bring in your calendar, Gmail, notes, and more.

- **Wake word** — Say "Stella" and start talking. No buttons, no typing needed.
- **Automate your routine** — Set up daily check-ins, reminders, and recurring tasks in plain English.
- **Manage your files** — Search, organize, read, and edit files on your computer.
