I'm up 22% on NVDA and it's now 40% of my portfolio. Should I trim some?

When a single name grows past roughly 25–30% of a portfolio, the position is driving most of your risk, not just your upside. Trimming part of it back toward your target weight locks in some of that gain and reduces how much a single earnings miss can hurt you.
