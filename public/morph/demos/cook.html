I have chickpeas, spinach, and half a can of tomatoes. What can I make for dinner?

That is the start of a lovely chickpea and spinach stew. Soften an onion and some garlic in olive oil, stir in the tomatoes with a pinch of cumin and smoked paprika, then add the drained chickpeas and let it simmer for ten minutes.

Fold the spinach through at the end until it just wilts, finish with lemon and salt, and serve over rice or with warm bread. It comes together in about twenty-five minutes.
