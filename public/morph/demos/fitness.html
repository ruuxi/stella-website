What weekly mileage should I start with for a spring marathon?

For a spring race, a gentle on-ramp is around 15–20 miles a week, spread over three or four runs. Keep most of them easy and conversational, add one slightly longer weekend run, and only grow your weekly total by about ten percent at a time.

Once that feels comfortable, we can layer in a weekly tempo session and start building toward your long run.
