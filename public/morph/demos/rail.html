When's the best low tide to explore the tide pools at Pillar Point?

Aim for a negative low tide in the early morning, ideally below −0.5 ft, when the most life is exposed and the light is soft. This weekend, Saturday around 7:40 a.m. looks ideal, with the water dropping to roughly −1.2 ft.

Bring sturdy shoes and tread gently — sea stars and anemones are easy to miss underfoot.
